# Bronco Distribution Design System - "Terminal Prime" (Light Ops Variant)

## Philosophy
- **Precision:** Every pixel serves a purpose. High data density where appropriate.
- **Industrial:** Brutalist influence but refined. Sharp edges, technical typography.
- **Speed:** Interactions are immediate. Transitions are fast (<200ms) and linear or slight ease-out.
- **Beyond Human:** The interface feels like it is generated by a superior intelligence for maximum efficiency.
- **Light Ops:** Unlike traditional dark terminals, this variant operates in "Daylight Mode" for maximum clarity in high-glare warehouse environments.

## Color Palette

### Backgrounds
- **BG Main (`bg-terminal-black`):** `#F9FAFB` - Cool Light Gray. The foundation.
- **BG Panel (`bg-terminal-panel`):** `#FFFFFF` - Pure White. Card surfaces.
- **BG Surface (`bg-terminal-surface`):** `#F3F4F6` - Inputs, secondary panels.
- **BG Highlight (`bg-terminal-highlight`):** `#E5E7EB` - Hover states.

### Borders & Lines
- **Border Dim (`border-terminal-border`):** `#E5E7EB` - Subtle structural dividers.
- **Border Active (`border-terminal-active`):** `#D1D5DB` - Focused inputs, active cards.

### Typography
- **Text Primary (`text-terminal-white`):** `#111111` - Almost Black. High contrast reading.
- **Text Secondary (`text-terminal-dim`):** `#6B7280` - Cool Gray 500. Supporting text.
- **Text Technical (`text-terminal-tech`):** `#9CA3AF` - Cool Gray 400. Metadata, labels.

### Accents
- **Accent Primary (`text-bronco-orange`):** `#FF5500` - Critical actions, alerts, "hazard" aesthetics.
- **Accent Secondary (`text-bronco-blue`):** `#00F0FF` - Optional indicators (use sparingly).

## Typography
- **Display Font:** `Inter` (or variable sans).
  - Tracking: `-0.05em` (Tighter for impactful headings).
  - Transform: `Uppercase` often used for headers.
- **Mono Font:** `JetBrains Mono`, `Roboto Mono`, or System Mono.
  - Usage: Product SKUs, Prices, Tech Specs, 'System Status' indicators.

## UI Tokens
- **Radius:** `rounded-none` or `rounded-sm` (2px). No large curves.
- **Shadows:** Sharp, hard shadows (`shadow-hard`) defined as `#121212` or `#A1A1AA` depending on context, but minimal usage.
- **Glassmorphism:** `backdrop-blur-md` with `bg-white/80`. Sleek, clean.

## Layout Principles
- **Grid Systems:** Visible or implied 12-column grids.
- **Spacing:** Compact but legible.
- **Decor:** 1px lines (`.bg-terminal-border`), crosshairs, corner brackets.

## Component Examples

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid Orange (`#FF5500`) text black, sharp corners.
- **Secondary:** Outline Gray (`border-terminal-border`), text dark, sharp corners.

### Cards
- White background (`#FFFFFF`).
- 1px Border (`#E5E7EB`).
- Hover: Border Color Shift to Orange or Dark Gray.
